auditions

Did you come out of the womb doing improv routines? Are you new to the scene but interested in Child's Play because we're so charming, fresh, intelligent, and dashing? Would you rather just spend 45 minutes laughing with us and having a great time? Sign up for auditions HERE!

No experience is necessary!!! We really mean it. We're looking for folks from all class years and bacgrounds, and during your audition, we will be looking at your attitude and willingness to learn and adapt, not how hard you can you make us laugh. We believe that everyone has what it takes to be a successful improviser! Please contact us if you have concerns or want advice going into your audition. We love you and want you to do your very best!
